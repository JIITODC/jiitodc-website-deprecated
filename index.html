About

Welcome to the JODC!

The JIIT Open Dev Circle is an open source initiative of Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida Sector-128. We guide students in their journey into the world of open source by contributing, collaborating, networking and learning together. We conduct meetups and workshops for the student body, and have dev-sprints and hackathons in store for the future.

How We Started

How we started the hub?

We started the hub ...

 

What were the requirements?

We at JIIT Open Source Dev Circle feel that we missed out on the exposure that was necessary for us to become an open source contributor right from the first year. We have used various resources available online and outside our campus to become Open Source contributors. We don't want other students of JIIT-128 to face the problems that we have faced.

 

What is the impact?

A lot of students are now aware of open source culture and many have started to contribute.

 

What are our plans and mission for the future?

We will have more workshops, meetups, devsprints, talks, projects, hackathon and ctf challenges.
